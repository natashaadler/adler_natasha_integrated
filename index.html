Our Values

Our Values Brief

Yoga is the science of self-awareness. Through this process we can access our greatest human potential and find self-empowerment, which will lead to an abundance of good in our lives. The following core values have become our foundation:

Our 6 Values

Self-Realization.
Yoga is the process of awakening to how perfect, complete, and powerful you already are.

Faith.
We trust that there is a greater awareness/presence that will support us on our path.

Freedom.
Realizing your complete inner freedom is the purpose of Yoga. We want you to be your true authentic self.

Connection.
This is an inclusive community where we support each other on this inner journey. We are all in this together

Peace.
When we quiet the noise of our lives, we can access the power of our innate creativity, and explore new potential.

Acceptance.
Know that who you are right now is perfect. Everyone is welcome and everyone is loved.
